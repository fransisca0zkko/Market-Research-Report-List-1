<p><ul><li>Arc Curing</li><li>LED Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV curing printing ink encompasses two primary types: arc curing and LED curing. Arc curing utilizes traditional mercury vapor lamps to emit UV light, effectively curing inks on various substrates with high energy output. In contrast, LED curing employs light-emitting diodes, offering energy efficiency and longer lifespan while generating less heat. Both methods enhance print durability, quick drying times, and vibrant colors, making them popular choices in commercial printing, packaging, and label production, each tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
