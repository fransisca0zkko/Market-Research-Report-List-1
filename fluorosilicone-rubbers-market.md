<p><p>The fluorosilicone rubbers market encompasses two primary types: High Temperature Vulcanization (HTV) and Room Temperature Vulcanization (RTV). HTV fluorosilicone rubbers are characterized by their ability to endure elevated temperatures and harsh environments, making them suitable for automotive and aerospace applications. In contrast, RTV fluorosilicone rubbers cure at ambient temperatures, providing user-friendly application and versatility for sealing and bonding in various industries, including electronics and consumer goods. Both types offer excellent chemical resistance and durability.</p></p>
