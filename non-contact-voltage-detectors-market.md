<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Magnetic Induction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact voltage detectors are essential tools that identify the presence of voltage without direct contact with live wires. The market includes two main types: capacitive and magnetic induction detectors. Capacitive detectors sense voltage through the electric field generated around live conductors, offering precise measurements for a variety of applications. Magnetic induction detectors, on the other hand, detect the electromagnetic field produced by electrical currents, making them ideal for assessing voltage levels in a broader range of environments. Both types enhance safety and efficiency in electrical maintenance.</p></p>
