<p><ul><li>Film Grade</li><li>Extrusion Coating Grade</li><li>Dispersible Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EAA adhesive market is segmented into three main types: Film Grade, Extrusion Coating Grade, and Dispersible Grade. Film Grade EAA adhesives are primarily used in applications that require strong bonding with flexibility, ideal for packaging and lamination. Extrusion Coating Grade is tailored for coating substrates during the extrusion process, providing excellent adhesion and moisture resistance, suitable for food packaging. Dispersible Grade EAA adhesives are designed for applications where easy dispersion is crucial, often utilized in water-based formulations for various industries.</p></p>
