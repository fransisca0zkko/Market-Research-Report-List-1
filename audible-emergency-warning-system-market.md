<p><strong>The Audible Emergency Warning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Omni Directional</li><li>Rotating Directional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Audible Emergency Warning System market is categorized into two main types: Omni-Directional and Rotating Directional systems. Omni-Directional systems emit sound in all directions, providing widespread alert coverage, making them ideal for large or open areas. In contrast, Rotating Directional systems focus sound in a specific direction using a rotating mechanism, allowing targeted alerts to particular zones while minimizing noise pollution in others. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing emergency communication and public safety during critical situations.</p></p>
