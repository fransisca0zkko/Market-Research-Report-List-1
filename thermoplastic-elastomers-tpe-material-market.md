<p><p>The thermoplastic elastomers (TPE) material market is primarily categorized into three types: Styrenic Block Copolymers (TPS or TPE-S), Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV), and Others. TPE-S offers excellent flexibility and durability, making it suitable for applications like soft-touch grips and packaging. TPVs combine rubber-like properties with the processability of plastics, ideal for automotive and industrial uses. The "Others" category encompasses various specialized TPE formulations, serving niche applications across diverse industries, from medical devices to consumer goods.</p></p>
