<p><strong>The I-line Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photopolymerization</li><li>Light Decomposition Type</li><li>Photocrosslinked</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The I-line photoresist market consists of three primary types: photopolymerization, light decomposition, and photocrosslinked photoresists. Photopolymerization involves the polymerization of monomers upon exposure to light, forming a solid pattern. In light decomposition, the photoresist breaks down into smaller molecules when exposed to light, creating a negative pattern. Photocrosslinked photoresists undergo a chemical reaction that links polymer chains, enhancing thermal and chemical stability. Each type serves distinct applications in semiconductor manufacturing and microfabrication processes.</p></p>
